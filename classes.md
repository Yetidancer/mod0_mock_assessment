***Class***: Chair<br>

**Attributes**:<br>
-legs (array)<br>
-occupied (boolean)<br>
-back (string)<br>
-cushions (array)<br>
-weight (integer)<br>

*Methods*<br>
-lean (modifies the back attribute)<br>
-legBreak (subtract a leg item from the legs array)<br>
-compress (modifies the cushions attribute)<br>
-clean (changes the cushions attribute)<br>
